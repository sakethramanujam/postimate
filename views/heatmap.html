Instructions to Try Heatmap Generation Experiment

Make sure you have finished the calibration procedure before visiting this page

  1. As the webpage loads, you'll be shown to random images on the left and right parts of your screen. Once you're ready, click on "Start Tracking" button.
  2. Unlike the calibration page there's no video feedback available, but there's a tiny red dot that follows your gaze. So, once you are done looking at the images, click on "Stop Tracking"
  3. Click on the "Show Heatmap" button to view the generated heatmap.
  4. Reload the page to repeat the experiment.
